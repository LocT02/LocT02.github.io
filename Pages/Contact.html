About Me

Loc Quang Trinh

Hey! I'm Loc, a general junior developer with piqued interest in web, gameplay & sound design. I graduated from UCI on June 2023 with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Game Science, and have been developing indie games since. My skillsets range from software development with Python3 to front-end development using HTML & CSS, to video game development using Unity, Ren'Py, and Unreal Engine 5.3. I enjoy a myriad of activities like composing or transcribing scores, almalgamating new and existing design ideas, and fantasizing living within a MMORPG-VR world. I mean, who doesn't want to level up and use kick-ass abilities?

With 3 published games in 3 years using Unity as my go-to engine, I recently started pursuing Unreal Engine 5 for its extensive use of high-end graphical fidelity using Nanite & Lumen, and blueprint development, in hope to expand my skillsets and knowledge in another engine.

Feel free to contact me for any regards; I'm always down to chat and try new things!
