Moonlight

Main Theme BGM for Hero, a singleplayer, narrative-driven 3rd-person currently in development. This piece was designed to provoke the theme of loss and growth; A hero doesn't have to be a person who shines like the sun, nor someone who fights evil. They can be the light that guides and shines your path when everything around you is dark and scary, thus Moonlight.

Games
C.C.C: Untitled

Prototype.
A hyper fast paced 2D action-platformer where you seamlessly swap between 3 unique characters with distinct abilities to achieve lengthy combos and rapid level clears.

Character Concepts-Mechanics
Conduit -- Quick and supportive
- Attack: ranged, builds stacks to be used by other characters
- Movement: downward dash, adds stacks if it goes through enemies
Control -- Slow with Crowd Control
- Attack: melee, knock back, AOE
- Movement: knock up self and enemies
- Consuming conduit stacks negates damage
Conjurer -- Damage
- Attack: melee, BIG damage, DOT, small AOE
- Movement: forward dash, adds DOT to enemies passed through
- Consuming conduit stacks increases damage
